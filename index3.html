Benzeny is the future

Benzeny is an appliction that specialises in fuel. It service all of the station companies,varoius companies as well as indiviuals.Benzeny provides detailed reports to all three parties and is the most convenient way of filling fuel

Project idea

We know well that it is necessary for our community to go along with the latest tools and new application in the world. So we have launched “benzeny” in the space of the Saudi community, because we need to serve the individuals, companies and the oil stations’ owners and gathering them all into one app. Top secret technical app seeks saving time and shorten paces and providing the highest level of financial security.

Benzeny smart chip

Benzeny smart chip is placed on the company's vehicle to ensure that the employee fills the company vehicle and not his own.This is the best way a company control and save money on fuel.Companies using Benzeny smart chip will not have to provide phones and internet their employees thus reducing cost.Benzeny smart chip is fast , secure and more convenient

Our Services


Benzeny serves all three of the various companies, station companies as well as the individuals.


Varoius companies
1) Benzeny is cost efficient as you will be able to set your budge at the start of the month
2) Benzeny is also time efficient as you would not spend hours and hours trying to finance petrol prices
3) Benzeny also provides monthly reports for each drivers containing which station the operation took place, the amount, petrol type and date/time.
4) Benzeny gives you complete control over your petrol finance in one single website.
Stations companies
1) Benzeny will provide daily/weekly/monthly reports to the stations in order to see the sales progress, how much liter was sold today whether it is Diesel,95 or 91.
2) The station company can also view reports of each worker and calculate his sales.
3) The station can reduce cash in the hands of their staff which often leads to theft or loss of cash since the worker will have to carry a big amount.
4) Benzeny will bring customers (various companies & individuals) to the stations.
Individuals
1) Benzeny provides monthly/weekly/daily reports so the user can track his/hers fuel expense.
2) The user can easliy select a stations with a desired service whether it is carwash, mechanic or a resturant.
3) User can easliy transfer balance to friends and family.
4) The user can fill thier vehicle without carrying any cash
